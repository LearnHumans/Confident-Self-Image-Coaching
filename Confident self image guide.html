My Story

In 2021, I tore the PCL in my left knee. Life was put on pause and I finished the rest of my freshman year bedridden in my dorm room. Full recovery took an entire year after the surgery. During that time, I gained weight, lost strength, and my confidence. Angry and frustrated with my situation, I struggled to make progress in anything I was doing. I felt unsure and nervous about everything. Even after making a successful recovery, I still felt like the same sad and stuck freshman lying in my dorm room. I saw the people around me moving on in their careers and realized I couldn't keep living like this. I had already been watching self-improvement content at the time, but all of it was just the same. I decided to read books on self-image and how to change not only physically but mentally as well. I got myself out of this year-long rut through incremental steps and daily habits. I got in shape again, got rid of my fear of going out, and became more comfortable with the uncomfortable. I gained my confidence again and now I teach people to do the same.
